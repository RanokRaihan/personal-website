# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 4, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how Ranok Raihan's personal portfolio website
(the "Site") handles your information. This is a simple portfolio site, so the
scope of data we handle is limited.

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us through the **contact
form**, which may include:

- Your name
- Your email address
- The message you send

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect
sensitive personal information.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to:

- Read and respond to your message.
- Follow up on any inquiry you have started.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for
marketing purposes.

## 3. Cookies and Analytics

The Site uses only what is necessary to function (for example, remembering your
light or dark theme preference). If any basic analytics are used, they are only
to understand general traffic and improve the Site — never to identify you
personally.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Site relies on a few third-party services to operate, such as image hosting
and the backend API that serves project and blog content. These providers
process data only as needed to deliver the Site. External links (like GitHub or
LinkedIn) are governed by their own privacy policies.

## 5. Data Retention

Messages sent through the contact form are kept only as long as needed to
respond and maintain a reasonable record of correspondence.

## 6. Your Choices

You can choose not to use the contact form. If you have already contacted us and
would like your message and details removed, email us and we will delete them.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be posted on this
page with a new date.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out at
[ranokraihan@gmail.com](mailto:ranokraihan@gmail.com).
